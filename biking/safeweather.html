The Rules of Cycling

  • 1. Wear your helmet.
  • 2. Keep your head up and look ahead, not at the ground.
  • 3. One person per bike.
  • 4. Ride in single file with space between bikes.
  • 5. Ride on the right side of the road, never against traffic.
  • 6. Plan ahead if you will ride in a group.
